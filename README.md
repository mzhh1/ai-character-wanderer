## Task

The final assignment for this semester is an exercise in "Human-AI Collaboration." The class of 30 students will be divided into 10 groups (3 students per group). There are 5 research topics available, with 2 groups assigned to each topic.

Core Philosophy: This experiment is not designed to prove how powerful AI is. Instead, through concrete practice, the goal is to understand the boundaries of AI and attempt to correct it when it makes mistakes.

Virtual Character Analysis

Background: Generating content for a single task is easy, but maintaining Character Consistency over the long term is extremely difficult. AI often changes personality mid-conversation or morphs facial features between images. This topic challenges AI’s "memory" and "logical consistency."

Objectives

Learn how to design complex Prompt Engineering to maintain coherence.

Experience the correction process in human-AI collaboration.

Requirements

Character Setup: Create a profile including not just a name, but age, MBTI type, catchphrases, physical features, and even childhood trauma. Write a full CV first.

Multimodal Shaping:

Text: Have the AI roleplay the character to write 5 diary entries from different dates, or conduct 5 rounds of deep conversation with group members (set traps to test if the persona breaks).

Image: Generate photos of the character in 5 different scenarios (eating, sports, sleeping, etc.). The face must look like the same person.

Audio/Motion: Generate a theme song or motion video that fits their personality.

Experiment Log: Detailed records of when the AI "did as you wished" and when it "spouted nonsense" or "changed faces." What instructions did you use to bring it back on track?

Deliverables

Character Archive: Setting sheets, diaries, lifestyle photo series, theme song.

Experiment Report: Focus on reviewing the "persona correction" process and summarize a "Guide to Maintaining AI Character Consistency."

## 课题：虚拟角色分析 (Virtual Character Analysis)

**背景：** 为单一任务生成内容非常简单，但长期保持**角色一致性（Character Consistency）**却极其困难。AI 经常在对话中途改变性格，或在不同图像间改变面部特征。本课题旨在挑战 AI 的“记忆力”与“逻辑一致性”。

### 目标

* 学习如何设计复杂的提示词工程（Prompt Engineering）以维持连贯性。
* 体验人机协作中的修正过程。

### 要求

1. **角色设定：** 创建一份角色档案，不仅包含姓名，还需包括年龄、MBTI 类型、口头禅、身体特征，甚至是童年阴影。首先需编写一份完整的**个人简历（CV）**。
2. **多模态塑造：**
* **文本：** 让 AI 扮演该角色撰写 5 篇不同日期的日记，或与小组成员进行 5 轮深度对话（设置陷阱以测试人设是否会崩塌）。
* **图像：** 生成该角色在 5 种不同场景下的照片（如用餐、运动、睡觉等）。面部特征必须看起来是同一个人。
* **音频/动态：** 生成符合其性格的主题曲或动态视频。


3. **实验日志：** 详细记录 AI 何时“如你所愿”，以及何时“胡言乱语”或“变脸”。你使用了什么指令将其引导回正轨？

### 交付成果

* **角色档案库：** 设定表、日记、生活照系列、主题曲。
* **实验报告：** 重点回顾“人设修正”的过程，并总结出一份《AI 角色一致性维护指南》。
